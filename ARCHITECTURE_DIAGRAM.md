## Data Flow Details

### Input Processing
1. **User Profile Data**: Retrieved from authenticated user session
2. **Environmental Data**: User-provided location, weather, and season
3. **Intake Data**: Medicine and food consumption for the day
4. **Optional Context**: Symptoms, activities, and additional notes

### Analysis Engine
The AI analyzes the interplay between:
- **Prakriti** (Natural constitution) vs **Vikriti** (Current state)
- **Ritu** (Season) and **Desha** (Location) effects
- **Ahara** (Food) compatibility with dosha and season
- **Aushadha** (Medicine) interactions and timing
- **Dinacharya** (Daily routine) optimization

### Output Generation
Results are structured into six key areas:
1. **Overall Score**: Quick health status indicator
2. **Body Analysis**: Constitutional balance assessment
3. **Weather Impact**: Environmental health factors
4. **Food Analysis**: Dietary suitability and modifications
5. **Medicine Safety**: Interaction warnings and advisories
6. **Holistic Recommendations**: Integrated wellness guidance
